# GraphMaker

Beautiful, presentation-ready graphs from your Excel data. Zero installations, zero dependencies — just open and use.

## Features

- **Upload Excel/CSV** — Drag and drop .xlsx, .xls, or .csv files
- **Pivot Table Engine** — Group, aggregate (sum/avg/count/min/max), split by categories, and filter your data
- **16 Chart Types** — Bar, Horizontal Bar, Stacked Bar, Grouped Bar, Line, Area, Pie, Donut, Scatter, Radar, Treemap, Funnel, Heatmap, Bubble, Waterfall, and Gantt
- **PPT-Optimized Export** — Export at Full HD, 2K, 4K, or custom sizes with 1x/2x/3x scale for razor-sharp text in PowerPoint
- **Copy to Clipboard** — Paste directly into PowerPoint with one click
- **6 Color Palettes** — Professional, high-contrast palettes designed for presentations
- **Dark/Light/Transparent Backgrounds** — Match your slide theme
- **Fully Client-Side** — Your data never leaves your browser

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in any modern browser
2. Drop your Excel file or click "Try with sample data"
3. Drag columns to Group By, Values, and Split By zones
4. Pick a chart type and customize colors, fonts, labels
5. Export as PNG or SVG at your desired resolution

## Tech Stack

- [Apache ECharts](https://echarts.apache.org/) — Chart rendering (SVG mode for crisp text)
- [SheetJS](https://sheetjs.com/) — Excel/CSV parsing
- Pure HTML/CSS/JS — No build tools, no frameworks, no npm
